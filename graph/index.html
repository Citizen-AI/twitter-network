Whose tweets do New Zealand MPs like?

Who are our MPs paying attention to? Who are they influenced by? Which MPs interact ‘across the isle’?

Connections show likes. People’s photos grow in proportion to the number of MPs who've liked their tweets. MPs are coloured by political party where that was easy to deduce from their Twitter bios.

Hover over people to see their connections. Hover over connections to see the last tweet liked. Drag people around to rearrange. Connections grow thicker with the number of tweets liked. Pinch or scroll to zoom. People with fewer than four favourited tweets have been hidden to simplify the chart.
